What's Holistic Therapy?

In a world where we are tired and taken over by daily routine, often what we need is to stop for a moment, analyze our problems and reconnect with our essence.

Based mainly on methods of relaxation, energy control and changes in lifestyle, Holistic Therapy helps us to observe and reflect on different aspects of our lives.

From this observation, surely, positive solutions and results will happen, promoting health and preventing diseases that compromise the integrity of our body-mind.

Reiki

Reiki is a form of holistic therapy referred to as energy healing that can be applied on site or remotely. The technique involves transferring universal energy from the practitioner’s hands to their patient promoting, energetic reorganization, healing, protection, among other benefits.
